Warehouse H&S Digital Audit Checklist

A comprehensive warehouse health and safety audit checklist aligned with UK HSE guidance and the Health and Safety at Work Act 1974. Complete digitally or print for your records.

Free Resource Printable UK HSE Aligned HSWA 1974
Important: This checklist is a practical aid for internal audits and does not constitute legal advice. It is not a substitute for a formal risk assessment under the Management of Health and Safety at Work Regulations 1999. Always consult a qualified H&S professional for your specific premises and activities.

🔥 Section 1 — Fire Safety
Item Pass / Fail / N/A Action Required / Notes
Fire risk assessment is current and reviewed within last 12 months
Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005, Art. 9
Fire exits are clearly marked, unobstructed and operational
RRFSO 2005, Art. 14
Fire extinguishers are in correct locations, in-date, and visually inspected
BS 5306-3; annual service record on site
Fire alarm system tested weekly; records maintained
BS 5839; RRFSO 2005
Fire evacuation procedure is displayed and staff have been drilled in last 12 months
RRFSO 2005, Art. 21
Flammable/hazardous materials stored correctly and away from ignition sources
DSEAR 2002; COSHH 2002
🏋️ Section 2 — Manual Handling
Item Pass / Fail / N/A Action Required / Notes
Manual handling risk assessment completed and documented
Manual Handling Operations Regulations 1992
All warehouse staff have received manual handling training (records on file)
MHOR 1992, Reg. 4
Mechanical aids (pallet trucks, sack trucks, lifts) available and in good repair
PUWER 1998
Maximum load weights are labelled on racking and shelving
PUWER 1998; HSWA 1974
🚜 Section 3 — Forklift Trucks & Materials Handling Equipment (MHE)
Item Pass / Fail / N/A Action Required / Notes
All FLT operators hold a valid RTITB / ITSSAR licence for the truck type operated
PUWER 1998; HSE ACOP L117
Pre-shift FLT checks completed and recorded daily
PUWER 1998, Reg. 6
Pedestrian and FLT traffic segregated with clear markings and barriers
Workplace (H,S&W) Regs 1992, Reg. 17
FLT LOLER thorough examination is current (every 12 months for mast/attachments)
LOLER 1998, Reg. 9
Speed limits are posted and enforced within the warehouse
HSE ACOP L117
🗄️ Section 4 — Racking & Storage
Item Pass / Fail / N/A Action Required / Notes
Racking inspected by a competent person in the last 12 months (SEMA-compliant)
PUWER 1998; HSE INDG271; SEMA CoP
Damaged racking is tagged, taken out of service and reported
SEMA CoP; PUWER 1998
Safe working load (SWL) is displayed on all racking bays
HSWA 1974; PUWER 1998
Racking is anchored to floor and free-standing where required
SEMA CoP
🦺 Section 5 — PPE & Protective Clothing
Item Pass / Fail / N/A Action Required / Notes
PPE requirements identified via risk assessment and documented
PPE at Work Regulations 2022 (updated)
Safety footwear (steel-toecap) worn by all staff in operational areas
PPE at Work Regs 2022
Hi-visibility vests worn in FLT operating areas
PPE at Work Regs 2022; HSE ACOP L117
PPE is stored correctly, inspected regularly and replaced when defective
PPE at Work Regs 2022, Reg. 7
🧹 Section 6 — Housekeeping & Workplace Conditions
Item Pass / Fail / N/A Action Required / Notes
Walkways and emergency routes are clear of obstructions at all times
Workplace (H,S&W) Regs 1992, Reg. 12
Floors are clean, dry and free from trip hazards; spillage procedure in place
Workplace (H,S&W) Regs 1992, Reg. 12
Adequate lighting in all operational areas and loading bays
Workplace (H,S&W) Regs 1992, Reg. 8
Temperature is maintained at a reasonable level (min. 16°C for sedentary, 13°C for active work)
Workplace (H,S&W) Regs 1992, Reg. 7
Welfare facilities (toilets, hand-washing, rest area) are available and maintained
Workplace (H,S&W) Regs 1992, Regs 20–25
🩺 Section 7 — First Aid & Incident Reporting
Item Pass / Fail / N/A Action Required / Notes
Adequate number of trained first aiders for shift size; certificates current
Health and Safety (First Aid) Regs 1981
First aid boxes stocked, in-date and inspected monthly
H&S (First Aid) Regs 1981
Accident book (or digital equivalent) is maintained and accessible
RIDDOR 2013; Social Security Admin Act 1992
RIDDOR-reportable incidents reported to HSE within required timescales
RIDDOR 2013 (7-day injuries within 15 days)
📋 Section 8 — General Compliance & Documentation
Item Pass / Fail / N/A Action Required / Notes
Employer's Liability Insurance certificate displayed on premises
Employers' Liability (Compulsory Insurance) Act 1969
Health and Safety Law poster is displayed in a prominent location
HSWA 1974; Health and Safety Info for Employees Regs 1989
Written H&S policy exists, signed by senior manager, and communicated to staff
HSWA 1974, s.2(3) — required for 5+ employees
Suitable and sufficient risk assessments exist for all significant workplace hazards
Management of H&S at Work Regs 1999, Reg. 3
Induction training records are held for all staff and contractors
HSWA 1974, s.2(2)(c)
