Affiliate & Commercial Disclosure

We believe you deserve to know exactly how OperationsEdge is funded and what that means for the content you read.

ℹ️
Summary: Some links on this site earn us a commission if you sign up for a paid plan. This never influences our scores or verdicts — we follow strict editorial independence standards.

Who we are

OperationsEdge (operationsedge.co.uk) is an independent website that reviews and compares SaaS tools for UK operations managers, warehouse managers and logistics professionals. The site is run by operations professionals based in the United Kingdom.

How this site is funded

OperationsEdge is currently free to read and operates without active commercial partnerships or affiliate arrangements. All external links to software vendors are standard editorial links — we do not currently earn commission or receive payment when you click them or sign up to any products.

We intend to enter into affiliate arrangements with software vendors in the future. When we do, this page will be updated to reflect those relationships clearly before any affiliate links go live on the site.

Our editorial independence policy

We take editorial independence seriously. Our content is not influenced by vendors, and our reviews reflect our honest assessment of each product. Specifically:

  • We do not accept payment for positive reviews or higher scores.
  • We do not allow vendors to review, approve or alter our editorial content prior to publication.
  • Our scoring criteria are consistent across all reviews.
  • We review tools regardless of whether any commercial relationship exists or is planned.

Compliance with UK regulations

When commercial arrangements are in place, disclosures will be made in accordance with:

  • UK Consumer Protection from Unfair Trading Regulations 2008
  • UK Advertising Standards Authority (ASA) guidelines on online endorsements
  • Competition and Markets Authority (CMA) guidance on online reviews (2021)

Sponsored content

We do not currently accept sponsored posts or paid editorial placement. If this changes in the future, any sponsored content will be clearly and prominently labelled as "Sponsored" or "Advertisement" at the top of the page.

Review samples and free access

Software vendors may occasionally provide us with free access to their platforms for review purposes. Where this has occurred, it is disclosed within the relevant review. Receiving free access does not guarantee a positive review or a higher score.

Pricing and feature accuracy

We make every effort to keep pricing information and feature descriptions accurate and up to date. However, SaaS pricing and features change frequently. Always verify current pricing and terms directly with the vendor before making a purchasing decision. Pricing noted on this site is provided for indicative comparison purposes only.

No investment or professional advice

Nothing on this site constitutes legal, financial, or professional advice. Our reviews and comparisons are for informational purposes only. You should conduct your own due diligence before purchasing any software product and, where appropriate, take independent professional advice.

Questions about this disclosure

If you have any questions about our commercial relationships, how a particular review was conducted, or our editorial independence policy, please contact us at hello@operationsedge.co.uk. We're happy to provide full transparency.

Last updated: April 2026. We review and update this disclosure page when our commercial arrangements change.
