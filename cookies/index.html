Cookie Policy

What cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them. Last updated: April 2026.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet or phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, to remember your preferences, and to provide information to website owners about how their site is used.

Cookies are not programs and cannot carry viruses or access information on your device beyond what the website sets.

2. Our approach to cookies

OperationsEdge is a static content website. We aim to keep our use of cookies minimal and to be transparent about any cookies in use. This policy is compliant with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR) and UK GDPR.

3. Cookies we use

Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the Website to function. They do not collect personal information and cannot be switched off. Under PECR, strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent.

Analytics cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with the Website — for example, which pages are visited most, how long visitors stay, and where they come from. This helps us improve the site.

Analytics cookies require your consent under PECR. We will implement a cookie consent mechanism before enabling any analytics cookies. At present, no analytics cookies are active on this site.

When analytics are implemented, this policy will be updated with full details including cookie names, durations and the analytics provider in use.

Third-party and embedded content cookies

We do not currently embed third-party content (such as YouTube videos or social media widgets) that would set third-party cookies.

We link to external websites (software vendor sites) but clicking a link takes you to that website — their own cookies are governed by their own privacy policies, not ours.

4. Cookies set by our hosting provider (Netlify)

Our website is hosted by Netlify. Netlify may set cookies for infrastructure purposes (such as load balancing and form handling). These are strictly necessary cookies. For details, see Netlify's Privacy Policy.

5. Google Fonts

This Website loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect limited technical data (such as your IP address) when your browser requests font files. Google states that this data is not used to build profiles or target advertising. For details, see Google Fonts privacy information.

No cookies are set by this font request.

6. How to control cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies that have been set
  • Allow, block or delete cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies from all websites
  • Delete all cookies when you close the browser

Instructions for the most common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some websites (including form submissions on this site).

You can also opt out of interest-based advertising across many networks at youronlinechoices.com.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as the Website develops and as we introduce new features or services. We will update the "Last updated" date when we do. We encourage you to check this page periodically.
