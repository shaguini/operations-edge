Important information about the basis of content published on OperationsEdge.co.uk.

Last updated: April 2026

General disclaimer

The information published on OperationsEdge.co.uk is provided for general informational and educational purposes only. All content is written in good faith and based on personal professional experience, hands-on product evaluation, and publicly available information at the time of publication.

Nothing on this site constitutes professional legal, financial, regulatory, or compliance advice. If you are making significant operational, commercial, or procurement decisions, you should seek independent professional advice appropriate to your specific circumstances.

Basis of content

Content on this site — including reviews, comparisons, guides, and case studies — is based on:

  • Personal experience managing warehouse and logistics operations in the UK over a period of more than 10 years
  • Hands-on evaluation of software products using free trials, demo environments, and where possible, real operational use
  • Anonymised operational data and outcomes from situations I have worked through directly
  • Publicly available product documentation, pricing pages, and vendor materials at the time of writing

Where content is based on anonymised operational scenarios, identifying details have been changed or removed. Any resemblance to specific organisations, people, or incidents is coincidental.

Accuracy and currency

Software products change frequently — pricing, features, and integrations can all be updated without notice. While I aim to keep reviews and comparisons current, I cannot guarantee that every piece of information on this site reflects the current state of a product.

Each article includes a publication or last-updated date. If you believe information is out of date or inaccurate, please contact me and I'll investigate and update it as soon as reasonably possible.

Affiliate relationships

This site currently has no active affiliate relationships. All links to software vendors are standard editorial links — no commission is earned when you click them or sign up for any product.

I intend to enter affiliate arrangements in the future. When that happens, this page and the affiliate disclosure page will be updated before any affiliate links go live. Affiliate status will never influence editorial scores or recommendations — tools will be assessed on the same criteria regardless of whether a commercial relationship exists.
