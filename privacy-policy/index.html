Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, store and protect your personal data. Last updated: April 2026.

Summary: We collect only what we need, we don't sell your data, and you have full rights under UK GDPR to access, correct or delete it. Contact us at hello@operationsedge.co.uk with any questions.

1. Who we are

OperationsEdge is a website operated at operationsedge.co.uk. We publish independent reviews and guides for UK operations managers, warehouse managers and logistics professionals.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, OperationsEdge is the data controller in respect of personal data collected through this website.

Contact: hello@operationsedge.co.uk

2. What personal data we collect

We collect personal data in the following circumstances:

Contact form

When you submit the contact form at /contact/, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name (optional)
  • The content of your message

Newsletter subscription

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect:

  • Your email address

Cookies and analytics

When you visit our website, we may collect technical data through cookies and analytics tools. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

Data you provide voluntarily

If you email us directly or otherwise correspond with us, we will retain that correspondence.

3. How we use your personal data

Purpose Data used Lawful basis
Responding to your enquiry via the contact form Name, email, message content Legitimate interests (responding to communication)
Sending the newsletter you subscribed to Email address Consent (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(a))
Improving the website via analytics Anonymised/aggregated usage data Legitimate interests
Complying with legal obligations As required Legal obligation (UK GDPR Art. 6(1)(c))

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Who we share your data with

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data to third parties.

We use the following third-party services that may process your data on our behalf as data processors:

  • Netlify — our website hosting provider, which processes contact form submissions. Netlify is headquartered in the US; data transfers are covered by appropriate safeguards. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Newsletter provider — if you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is stored with our email marketing provider. We will update this policy when a provider is in use.
  • Analytics — we may use an analytics service (such as Google Analytics or a privacy-first alternative) to understand how visitors use the site. See our Cookie Policy.

We may disclose personal data if required to do so by law, a court order, or a regulatory authority.

5. International data transfers

Some of the third parties listed above are based outside the UK. Where personal data is transferred outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA) or adequacy decisions under UK GDPR.

6. How long we keep your data

  • Contact form enquiries: retained for up to 2 years from the date of your message, or until you request deletion.
  • Newsletter subscribers: retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Analytics data: retained in anonymised/aggregated form; identifiable data (if any) retained for no longer than 26 months.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (Subject Access Request).
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to certain conditions.
  • Right to restrict processing — you can ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — where processing is based on consent or contract, you can ask us to provide your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time (e.g. by unsubscribing from our newsletter).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@operationsedge.co.uk. We will respond within one month of receiving your request, as required by UK GDPR.

8. Cookies

We use cookies on this website. For full details of what cookies we use and why, please see our Cookie Policy.

9. Children's data

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal data to us, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. How to complain

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your personal data, please contact us first at hello@operationsedge.co.uk and we will do our best to resolve it.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK's data protection regulator:

  • Website: ico.org.uk
  • Helpline: 0303 123 1113
  • Address: Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire SK9 5AF
