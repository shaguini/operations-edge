Peak Period — Labour Gap Forecasting

The forecast said we needed
10 more people. Fast.

Workload was going up. The permanent team couldn't cover it. We had to bring in a group of agency starters within a short window.

The problem most operations hit at this point: they solve the recruitment problem and forget the onboarding problem. People arrive. They're put on the floor. The permanent team gets overloaded covering for them. And you end up paying for headcount that isn't contributing.
