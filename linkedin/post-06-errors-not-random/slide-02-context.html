Warehouse Operation — Error Analysis

Error rates were acceptable.
That didn't mean we accepted them.

We had picking errors. Not at a rate that was causing operational crisis — but enough to investigate. When we pulled the WMS data, a pattern appeared immediately.

Errors were concentrated. Specific zones. Specific SKUs. Specific shifts. Not spread evenly across the operation like noise — clustered like signal.

Clustered errors have solvable root causes. That's the opportunity.
