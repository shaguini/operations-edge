What We Did 3 / 5

We categorised every error.
Then we stopped blaming people.

1
Pulled WMS error data and categorised by type

Wrong item. Wrong quantity. Missed item. Location error. Four types — mapped in Excel by shift, zone, and SKU.

2
Looked for concentration, not distribution

If errors were random, they'd be spread. Ours weren't. Two specific zones generated a disproportionate share. That's a layout or slotting problem — not an operator problem.

3
Ran structured observation sessions on the high-error zones

Using SafetyCulture checklists. Watching what actually happened at the pick face — not what we assumed was happening.

4
Applied fixes to verified causes — not assumptions

Zone lighting. Label clarity. Slotting of visually similar SKUs. Replenishment sequencing. Every fix tied to a specific finding.
