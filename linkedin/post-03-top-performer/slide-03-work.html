What We Found 3 / 5

Three behaviours.
All replicable.

1
Pick path discipline

This operator never deviated from their planned route. Fewer wasted steps, fewer location confusion errors. They treated the pick path as fixed — not a suggestion.

2
Controlled scanner rhythm

Deliberate, consistent confirmation pace — not rushing the scan. Fewer mis-scans meant fewer corrections, which meant more picks per hour overall.

3
Proactive replenishment timing

This operator flagged replenishment needs before their locations ran dry. Zero stops mid-pick for empty slots. Other operators were losing minutes here every shift.
