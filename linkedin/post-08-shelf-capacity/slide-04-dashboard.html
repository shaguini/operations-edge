Consequences of System–Physical Mismatch

Pick face overcrowding → visual confusion → wrong-item picks Products too close together at the pick face. Operator error that wasn't operator error.
Replenishment inconsistency → empty locations mid-pick When overfill caused product damage, effective capacity dropped further. Unplanned empty slots.
Fix: physical audit → WMS update → fill limit standardisation → quarterly review Error rate at affected locations dropped within 2 weeks of correction.
The WMS was set at go-live and never reviewed. That was the real gap.
