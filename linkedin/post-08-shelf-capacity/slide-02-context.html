The Situation 2 / 5

WMS Capacity vs Physical Reality

The system and the shelf
disagreed. The shelf won.

During root cause analysis on a cluster of wrong-item picks, we applied the 5 Whys to one recurring location. Every time, the error linked back to a specific shelf that was consistently overcrowded.

The WMS said one thing about shelf capacity. The physical shelf held less.

The system had never been updated to reflect the physical reality. Replenishment was filling to system capacity. The shelf was overflowing. Pick face was a mess. Errors were inevitable.
