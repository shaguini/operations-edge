The 5 Categories 3 / 5

Every recurring error gets
mapped across all five.

P
People — behaviour, habits, training gaps, fatigue

What the operator is doing — scan rhythm, pick path adherence, confirmation habits.

P
Process — confirmation steps, scan rhythm, pick path design

How the operation is structured — where errors are baked into the process itself.

E
Equipment — scanner reliability, label readability, hardware condition

Tools that fail or mislead — mis-reads, worn labels, unreliable scan confirmation.

E
Environment — lighting, signage, aisle layout, overcrowding

Physical conditions at the pick face. Often the actual cause of "operator" errors.

M
Materials — packaging similarity, barcode placement, product presentation

How products look and behave at the pick face. High-risk when similar SKUs are adjacent.
