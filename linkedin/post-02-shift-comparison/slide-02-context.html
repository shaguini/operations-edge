Picking Operations — Shift Analysis

Same team. Same warehouse.
Different results.

When we broke down LPH by shift, the pattern was consistent. Late shift outperformed early shift — week after week.

The default assumption in most warehouses would be: different staff quality. That's the wrong answer. Both shifts had similar operator profiles.

The difference was in the conditions they were working in.
