The 5 Non-Negotiables 3 / 5

Five things. Every starter.
No exceptions.

1
Emergency exit locations and muster point — walk them there

Not "they're over there." Walk the route. Point to the muster point. Make it physical memory, not verbal.

2
Manual handling sign-off — dated, witnessed, filed

Not a box-tick. A demonstrated understanding. Filed with a date and witness name.

3
PPE confirmation — they have it, it fits, they're wearing it

Confirmed before they enter the operational area. Not assumed.

4
Site rules acknowledgement — phones, restricted areas, break protocol

Written acknowledgement. Especially phones in pick zones and restricted area boundaries.

5
Reporting line — a name, not a job title

"Speak to your supervisor" is useless on day one. Give them a specific named person.
