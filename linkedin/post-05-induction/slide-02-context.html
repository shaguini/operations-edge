Agency Induction — Where It Goes Wrong

Induction gets treated
as paperwork. It isn't.

Most warehouse inductions are designed to produce a signed form. Emergency exits are mentioned. Manual handling is "covered." PPE is handed over. Then the new starter is sent to the floor.

The problem: forms don't prevent incidents. Understanding does. A signed form means someone was in the room. It doesn't mean they know what to do when the fire alarm goes off, or who to report a near-miss to.
